## üõ†Ô∏è **Setting Up Your First Ad**

### **Quick Start (5 Minutes):**

1. **Navigate to Admin Dashboard**
   - Go to: `yourwebsite.com/admin/ad-config`
   - You'll see your advertising control panel

2. **Add New Placement**
   - Click the "+ Add New Placement" button
   - Fill in the basic information

3. **Essential Fields to Fill:**
   - **Title**: What the ad is called
   - **Description**: Brief explanation of the offer
   - **CTA Button**: What the button says (e.g., "Get Started")
   - **CTA URL**: Where people go when they click
   - **Placement**: Where on your site to show it
   - **Priority**: How important this ad is (1 = highest)

4. **Save & Launch**
   - Click save - your ad is now live!
   - It will automatically appear in the chosen location

### **What You Need From Advertisers:**

- **Ad Image** (usually 300x250 pixels for sidebars)
- **Ad Text** (title, description, button text)
- **Landing Page URL** (where clicks go)
- **Campaign Duration** (how long to run)
- **Targeting Preferences** (which pages to show on)
- **Budget** (how much they want to spend)

## üí∞ **Understanding Revenue**

### **Revenue Streams:**

1. **Display Advertising** - $2,000+ monthly target
2. **Affiliate Marketing** - $3,000+ monthly target
3. **Partner Content** - $1,000+ monthly target
4. **Newsletter Monetization** - $350+ monthly target

### **CPM (Cost Per Mille) Explained:**
- **CPM** = Cost per 1,000 impressions (views)
- **Example**: $20 CPM means you earn $20 for every 1,000 people who see the ad
- **Your Rates**: $5-30 CPM depending on placement and audience quality

### **Revenue Calculation Example:**
```
Monthly Page Views: 100,000
Ad Placement: Premium Sidebar ($25 CPM)
Calculation: (100,000 √∑ 1,000) √ó $25 = $2,500
```

## üîß **Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues & Solutions:**

#### **Ad Not Showing:**
1. Check if ad is active in admin dashboard
2. Verify placement settings
3. Check targeting rules
4. Ensure ad hasn't expired

#### **Low Click-Through Rate:**
1. Review ad copy and imagery
2. Check landing page relevance
3. Adjust targeting parameters
4. Test different CTA buttons

#### **Revenue Below Expectations:**
1. Analyze high-performing placements
2. Optimize underperforming slots
3. Adjust CPM rates
4. Increase premium placement usage

### **Emergency Procedures:**
- **All Ads Down**: Use "Emergency Disable" button
- **Performance Issues**: Check admin dashboard metrics
- **Technical Problems**: Restart your website if needed
