### 2. Purpose and Scope

#### Purpose
This policy establishes the framework for protecting AI Buzz Media's information assets, systems, and data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

#### Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and temporary workers
- All company systems, networks, and data
- All company facilities and locations
- All third-party vendors and service providers
- All company-owned and personal devices used for business purposes

### 3. Information Classification

#### 3.1 Public Information
- **Definition:** Information that can be freely shared with the public
- **Examples:** Published articles, press releases, marketing materials
- **Handling:** No special protection required

#### 3.2 Internal Information
- **Definition:** Information intended for internal use only
- **Examples:** Internal communications, project plans, employee directories
- **Handling:** Access limited to employees and authorized contractors

#### 3.3 Confidential Information
- **Definition:** Sensitive information that could harm the company if disclosed
- **Examples:** Financial data, customer information, source code, security configurations
- **Handling:** Access limited to authorized personnel, encryption required

#### 3.4 Restricted Information
- **Definition:** Highly sensitive information requiring maximum protection
- **Examples:** Encryption keys, security credentials, legal documents
- **Handling:** Access limited to specific authorized personnel, additional security measures

### 4. Access Control Policy

#### 4.1 User Access Management
- All users must have unique accounts
- Passwords must meet complexity requirements
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all accounts
- Access granted based on principle of least privilege
- Regular access reviews conducted quarterly

#### 4.2 Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- No dictionary words or common patterns
- Changed every 90 days
- No password reuse for 12 months

#### 4.3 Account Management
- New accounts require management approval
- Disabled accounts removed within 30 days
- Inactive accounts reviewed monthly
- Privileged accounts require additional approval

### 5. Data Protection Policy

#### 5.1 Data Encryption
- All sensitive data encrypted at rest
- All data encrypted in transit
- Encryption keys managed securely
- Regular encryption audits conducted

#### 5.2 Data Backup
- Automated backup systems implemented
- Backups encrypted and stored securely
- Backup integrity verified regularly
- Disaster recovery procedures documented

#### 5.3 Data Retention
- Data retention schedules established
- Regular data cleanup procedures
- Secure data disposal methods
- Compliance with legal requirements

### 6. Network Security Policy

#### 6.1 Network Access
- Firewall protection on all networks
- Network segmentation implemented
- VPN required for remote access
- Guest network isolated from corporate network

#### 6.2 Wireless Security
- WPA3 encryption required
- Guest wireless network separate
- Wireless access points secured
- Regular wireless security audits

#### 6.3 Network Monitoring
- Intrusion detection systems deployed
- Network traffic monitored
- Suspicious activity alerts configured
- Regular network security assessments
